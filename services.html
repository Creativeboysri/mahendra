OUR SERVICES

We deliver expert cutting solutions for commercial, industrial, and residential projects.

Transmission Line Construction (132 kV – 800 kV)

  • Survey, soil investigation & route finalization
  • Tower foundation works (all types)
  • Tower erection
  • Conductor stringing (single/double/multi circuit)
  • Special stringing with TSE machine
  • ERS tower erection
  • Shutdown & live-line works
  • Diversions, LILO works & re-routing
  • Emergency restoration works (cyclone / disaster response)

Substation Construction (132 kV / 220 kV / 400 kV / 765 kV)

  • Equipment erection
  • Transformer erection (100 MVA / 160 MVA, etc.)
  • Busbar works & bay extensions
  • Cable laying (control & power)
  • Earthing systems
  • Lighting protection
  • Control room civil works
  • Testing & commissioning support
  • Yard development

Solar Transmission EPC

  • 30 MW / 40 MW / 90 MW solar evacuation line works
  • 132 kV bay works for solar substations
  • Transmission line works for solar developers
  • Testing & commissioning of solar evacuation systems

O&M (Operation & Maintenance)

  • Conductor/insulator replacement
  • Stringing repairs
  • Tower strengthening & painting
  • Emergency fault rectification
  • Preventive maintenance for TL & SS

Industrial Electrical Works

  • Power & control cable laying
  • LT panels, ACDB, DCDB, battery chargers
  • GI support structures, clamps & connectors
  • Cable trays, lighting & auxiliary systems
  • Earthing systems

Design & Engineering Support

  • Foundation design assistance
  • Technical documentation & as-built drawings
