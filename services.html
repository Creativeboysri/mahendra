CORE DRILLING

DEMO SAWING

Specializing in precise and efficient demolition sawing, our Melbourne-based team delivers expert cutting services.

RING SAWING

Precision wall sawing services designed for clean, accurate cuts in concrete, brick, and masonry structures.

WIRE SAWING

Preferred method for cutting virtually any size of heavily reinforced concrete.

ROAD SAWING

Suitable to cut horizontal flat surfaces such as floors, bridge decks, bitumen and pavements.
