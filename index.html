Mahendra Constructions has over 40 years of proven execution experience across major transmission utilities and EPC companies in Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, and across India. Our team has successfully delivered works from 132 kV to 800 kV, including transmission lines, substations, solar evacuation systems, and emergency restoration works.

We are known for precision, reliability, and timely execution, with experience ranging across transmission, substation, solar evacuation systems, and O&M works.
